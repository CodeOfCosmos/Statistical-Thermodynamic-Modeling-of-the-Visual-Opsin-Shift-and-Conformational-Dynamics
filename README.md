# Statistical-Thermodynamic-Modeling-of-the-Visual-Opsin-Shift-and-Conformational-Dynamics
a discrete, two-state thermodynamic model to quantify the macromolecular free energy transition from inactive Rhodopsin to active Metarhodopsin II.Formulated and numerically integrated canonical partition functions (\(Z_{\text{cis}}\) and \(Z_{\text{trans}}\)) in Python over a defined reaction coordinate (ξ) to track macromolecular population distributions.Simulated the biological "opsin shift" by incorporating a 22.0 \(k_{B}T\) steric work function (\(W_{\text{push}}\)) representing the mechanical push of all-trans retinal, demonstrating a complete spontaneous inversion of the net Helmholtz Free Energy (Δ F) from a stable positive baseline to a favorable negative driving force.
